EMUBT BOARD INSTALLATION MANUAL


1

This is your Digifant ECU, I tested at least three versions: DM, CP and B (purple one)







2

After opening the ECU you should see white plastic sheet and under it metal cover. If there is no metal cover you are not the first one who opens it :D





3

There should be screws on top of metal cover and on PCB as well, unscrew all of them and open it like below





4

Remove the metal cover, there should be Motorola microntroller and EEPROM chip





5

Remove the chip carefully with some flat small knife







6

Locate C68 capacitor on the board, you need to connect RX0 pad of EMUBT board with it like on the picture





7

Install the board in EEPROM socket, it will fit only in one position, check the picture below





8

Close the ECU, everything should fit nice :D
